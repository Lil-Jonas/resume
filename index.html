Experience

AI Engineer - Bank Al-Maghrib

During this 2-month internship at Bank Al-Maghrib, I developed an LLM-based chatbot capable of interacting in Moroccan dialect, optimized through collaboration with my supervisor and another engineer. The chatbot was deployed on an Azure VM after being fine-tuned in a Google Colab environment using its GPU capabilities for handling high computational needs. My work also involved following Agile practices and leveraging LangChain to ensure seamless chatbot operation offline.

I worked independently, honing my full-stack development skills.

July 2023 - August 2023

Software Development Engineer - 4D
